Ready for some Breakfast Recipes?

Ingredients: 2 slices of rye bread; 100 gr feta cheese, beetroots, & one hand of walnuts.

On a slice of rye bread spread beetroots, feta cheese and walnuts. Easy as that.

And, your breakfast is done! Enjoy!

Ingredients: 40 gr granola, tea-spoon of flaxseeds; 50 ml coconut milk; table-spoon of peanut buter; table-spoon of jam; hand of mango & raspberries.

Mix flaxseeds, granola, and coconut milk and top them with one table-spoon of peanut butter, table-spoon of jam, mango and raspberries.

The quantity of the ingredients really depends on your personal preferences. Deliciousness guaranteed!!

Ingredients: 1 pack of sausages, 2 eggs, 1 bell-pepper, 1 small tomato, 1 pack of bacon, 50 gr cottage cheese.

Firstly, we cut the bell pepper, the tomato, and the bacon and we fry them for approximately 7 minutes at a medium heat.

After they are fried we add them in a bowl in which we mix them with the cottage cheese and with the 2 eggs.

In a frying pan, we place the sausages in a squared form and inside of the squared area we add the mixture, we fry for about 5 minutes.

Voila, the breakfast is done.

Finally, we serve the breakfast with a bit of arugula and half an avocado.
