Ingredients: 200 gr polenta/cornmeal, 250 gr pork meat, 100 gr feta cheese, sour-cream, 1 onion, 1 can of tomato pasta,& salad.

Bring water and salt to a boil in a large saucepan; pour the cornmeal slowly into boiling water, whisking constantly until all cornmeal is stirred

in and there are no lumps.

Reduce heat to low and simmer, whisking often, until cornmeal starts to thicken, about 5 minutes. And, polenta is done!

For the sauce on the side, just fry pork meat, onion and tomato pasta. Simple as that.

In the end, serve the polenta and the sauce with sour cream, feta cheese and salad of your choice.

Ingredients: 250 gr of chicken breasts, half an onion, bell pepper, & 1 pack of spinach.

I am hoping that anyone reading this blog, already knows how to boil some pasta. That's the first step of this recipe.

The second step is to cut the onion, bell pepper and the chicken breast into smaller pieces.

After everything is cut, you can fry all of those ingredients for about 10-15 minutes at a medium heat and cover them with a lid.

After 15 minutes, add a whole pack of spinach, add spices, mix and fry for additional 5-7 minutes. Voila. The sauce is done.

Serve the food in a plate and enjoy the meal!
