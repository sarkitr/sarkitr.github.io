Ready for some Dinner Recipes?

Ingredients: asparagus, several slices of bacon, & cheese.

Preheat an oven to 220 degrees C. Place the asparagus into a baking tray, and drizzle with the olive oil.

Bake for 10 minutes and then sprinkle with cheese, salt, pepper, and slices of bacon.

Then bake for about 5-7 minutes more and the dinner is ready.

On some crackers spread hummus, while on other crackers spread tuna, cheese and tomatoes. Serve them with some olives and cheese.
